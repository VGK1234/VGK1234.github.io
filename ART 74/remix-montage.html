Remix Montage

In my remix montage, I wanted to convey the message that digital techonology has a huge influence on the ways we communicate. Texting with trusted friends can be useful when done in moderation, however, if we do it more often than communicating in person, we are more likely to experience lonliness. In my montage the people texting are frustrated, while the people outside are happy and cheerful together. This compares how people feel communicating behind a screen and how people communicate in person.
