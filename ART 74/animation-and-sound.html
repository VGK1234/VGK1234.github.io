Animation & Sound

In this project, I tried to make a video of the remix montage. As you can see, both use the same exact images and backgrounds. The only difference is that some of the images are moving. The emojis are moving across the screen as people inside are walking around and texting. Later, the people outside slowly appear on the screen. In my sound montage, I tried to play the sound of a person typing on the keyboard to emphasize the frustration of people texting.
