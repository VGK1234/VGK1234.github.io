My Artist Statement

My name is Vinita Kulkarni and I am majoring in Digital Media Art. I have always been interested in the arts since I was a child. Since I was young, I used to love drawing, painting, and doing crafty things. I mostly loved to draw and I also used Paint software to doodle every now and then.

My interest in the Digital Arts began during my later high school years. At that time, I started taking a Web Design course and an Animation course. I learned how to create web sites using HTML and CSS, and how to create stories through animating characters and writing a storyboard.

Since I started taking classes at SJSU, I have learned how to expand my creative and imaginative side through using different software. I have learned the basics of how to use many Adobe Creative Cloud programs like Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Dreamweaver, Lightroom, and Premiere Pro. I also had the experiences of using Processing, Atom, and Github. Being a Digital Media major at SJSU has overall helped me cultivate more creative skills and become a better artist overall.

A portion of the work that I have done is based on technology and how it affects society.
