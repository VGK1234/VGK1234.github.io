Project 2: Video as Installation

This video illustrated how our emotions are effected by the internet and social media more than we think. When we see one negative post or bad news, we often tend to feel miserable for the rest of our day wherever we go. However, when we see one, or often multiple happy posts, our days often vastly improve. Today, technology plays a greater role in our moods and lives than ever before due to the creation and expansion of social media, so should balance the amount of negative and positive things that we see so we do not feel too miserable.
