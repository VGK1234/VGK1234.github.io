Project 1: The Internet and Security

In this project, I wanted to convey they message that our Internet data is always being tracked down. My video tries to show how whenever we search something up, we often encounter ads pertaining to what we search in other web pages. Not many people are aware of this and it is important that they know so that they will watch what they search.
